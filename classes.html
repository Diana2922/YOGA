Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
A 60 minute class of poses and slow movement that focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture), pranayama (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection. This class is intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga.
Vinyasa Yoga
A 60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement — you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses.While intended for intermediate to advanced students,beginners are welcomed to join in this class.
Restoractive Yoga
A 90 minute class taht features very slow movement and long poses.Restoractive yoga is useful in relieving stress and fostering a state of well-being. This calming and restoractive experience is suitable for students of any level of experience.
